About QuizBiz

QuizBiz is a cutting-edge platform designed to make quiz creation, participation, and management easy and enjoyable.

We use advanced AI algorithms to generate quizzes based on URLs and can create multiple types of questions. Whether you're a teacher, student, or just someone who loves quizzes, QuizBiz provides the tools you need to create and take fun and challenging quizzes.

We aim to create a community-driven platform where users can share their quizzes and learn from each other. Join us today and start exploring the world of quizzes!
